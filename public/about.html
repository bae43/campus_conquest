About

Gameplay Turn Based Strategy at Cornell

Campus Conquest is an online turn-based strategy game that takes place on a game board of Cornell University's campus. Players sign on as part of their respective colleges, and starting with their college's capital, design a strategy to conquer the rest of campus.

Web 3.0 Built from the ground up

The game engine is built with the latest technologies, enabling for powerful 3D graphics and smooth gameplay that has not even been possible until recently. Older versions lagged until everything was rebuilt with more efficient database handling, graphics engine, and websockets network communication.

Future Work To the Ivy League and Beyond

Campus Conquest is built on modular design. Its map creator allows for uploading custom models and designing a layout for any campus or other location. Other Ivies can be added in future work very quickly.
